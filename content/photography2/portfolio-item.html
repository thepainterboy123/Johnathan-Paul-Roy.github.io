In my experience painting, its good to know we are dealing with very real forces of nature. I grew up at Offit Strategic Air Commmand Base as a Child. Now I'm here in Colorado. I've done it all, painted police officers house, lawers house, governers house, many many high profile tech center homes. I painted FBI Homes, Doctor Homes, Worked in Secure Clearence Areas, and many commercial environments we don't need to reimagine. When we take this journey working together, it's almost never perfect until the very end. I'm really making my marks and paying attention what looks best to you. I'm listening and the World remains busy. When we go through these longer journey's minding the many many many different walks of life I rendered beautiful works in the past. There will be reflections, and my virtue is this, these reflections are intuitions, when they are intuitive to ideas you don't normally have, I believe those intuitions are destructive and Destructive intuitions are just a natural instinct a primal instinct. When you Working with Painting we are creating a new work of art not a previous work of art. My best virtue mentioning this is to Always Avoid Destructive Intuitions and Use Guided Intuition.
