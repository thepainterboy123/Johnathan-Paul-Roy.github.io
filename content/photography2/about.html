ABOUT US

Wide Range
of Coating Applications

RainCoat Painting offers a wide range of Interior and Exterior coating applications. Raincoat Painting Focuses on Residential Coating Applications, with Commercial Services Available upon request. We exercise Latex, Acrylic, and Oil Based Coating applications. We exercise Brush and Roll application. We prefer to assess application surfaces to approach applications with the right proceedure. We offer spray applications by Airless for Even Millage factors on the surface. Also Offering Capspray Applications for Fine Finish surfaces to achieve more flawless results on Metal Frames and Decor type surfaces. Utilizing the proper approach gives proper results, RainCoat includes professional masking and prep on all projects to give longlasting results and cleanability. With RainCoat you won't have to worry about intermediate surface failures and be able to focus on less maintainence in long term property maintence.

If Projects go over Expected Costs, Invoicing for Unexpected Costs will be discussed prior to the projects end date. And Unexpected Costs will be Invoiced as a Back Pay or Bill of Services at the End of the Project. These vantage points will be included in the proposal as a "Bill of Material and the Terms, Conditions, and Limitations" Utilizing RainCoats Painting and Staining Services.
